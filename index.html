Who Are We?

The one and only University of Illinois Sailing Club!

Open to both graduate and undergraduate students at University of Illinois, our club is comprised of sailors of all skill levels. Experienced skippers are welcome to try their hand at a regatta competing against various schools around the Midwest and even newcomers are allowed to participate as crew. Where Do We Sail?

Commodore Decature Yacht Club

We sail with the Commodore Decatur Yacht Club on Lake Decatur in Decatur, IL. It about a 40 minute drive from campus. The CDYC is always extremely welcoming to our club.
